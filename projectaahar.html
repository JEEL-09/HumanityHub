OUR PROJECT

"The economic challenges faced by many individuals, including migrant workers and street vendors, have disrupted their lives significantly. The difficult circumstances they find themselves in make it increasingly challenging to meet their basic needs. While these individuals may not be directly impacted by a pandemic, they struggle daily to alleviate hunger and poverty. As we enjoy the comforts of our homes during times of tranquility, it's essential to extend a helping hand to those less fortunate. At HumanityHub, we believe in the principle of lifting others as we rise. To support economically disadvantaged families, we have initiated a program to provide them with nutritious, home-cooked meals at a subsidized rate of Rs 50/- per person, sourced from local suppliers."
